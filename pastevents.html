Past Events

Here you can find a list of past events from the 2014-15 academic year. Find out about our current events at our Facebook page.

Copenhagen

2014-15 was a great year for ICDC and to end it on a high we went on our first ever tour to Copenhagen. The capital of simple, practical, and elegant Nordic design, Copenhagen has a lot to offer for anyone interested in design.
We explored the city by day and by night, by land and by sea, by bicycle and by me-mover.

ICDC Annual General Meeting

The time has come to pass the torch. The future of ICDC is in your sweaty palms. The Design Collective has grown massively this year... Perhaps a sign that Imperial is finally filling with some more-rad-than-average people? Who knows/cares. The point is, as an ICDC member your vote counts. Come and choose your future indie/hipster overlords.


Social: Thomas Heatherwick at the Doodle Bar

Thomas Heatherwick will be giving a short talk at Doodle Bar. Ever wanted to draw all over a wall whilst drinking a pint at the pub? The Doodle Bar provides the perfect watering hole for the savvy hipster inside us all. For Imperial students unfamiliar with the word 'social', it means giving up some work time to hang out with some like-minded individuals. Don't be boring. Come. With Friends.

Talk: Chris Wise Engineering & Emotion

Imperial College's inaugral Professor of Creative Design, Chris Wise (Expedition Engineering) will be giving a talk on 'Engineering & Emotion'. Free to members and non-members, so do invite everyone you know.

Talk: Engineering at Foster + Partners

Roger Ridsdill Smith of Foster + Partners will be giving a talk on 'Testing unknown ideas - The scope for creativity in Engineering'. This is our not-to-be-missed flagship event! Free to members and non-members, so invite all your friends!

Social: (Free) Pizza & Sketching

Please bring along something you'd like to sketch, or so that others can also sketch it. This event is FREE, but you need membership. Sign up so we know how much pizza to order!

Workshop: James Dyson Foundation Workshop

The James Dyson Foundation (JDF) are coming to Imperial to run an exclusive workshop! This is a team workshop and free to members and non-members. Bring your friends! Sign up on the Facebook event for further details.

Workshop & Social: Origami

Youtube origami celeb Rob Vaughan will be hosting a hour and a half session running us through the Japanese art form. No previous folding experience needed. This is your chance to get intimate with paper and hone your modelling skills.

Here are some videos to get you started.

Workshop: Introduction to Arduino

Run by the incredible Avi Braun; this 2-part workshop will get you started with everything Arduino. Learn to make little electronic devices, and be introduced to the world of microcontroller programming.

Check out the workshop materials.

Workshop: ICAH & ICDC Present 3D Printing

In glorious collaboration with IC Advanced Hackspace. This 3-part workshop will teach you everything you need to know to start designing for 3d printers. Come one, come all!

Have a look at the design brief.

Workshop: Can't sketch? Learn to sketch!

Every designer needs to be able to sketch their ideas; it is a vital way to communicate with a team. This workshop will teach you the very basics, starting by how to hold a pencil. Don't miss our first workshop for 2014-15!

Social: V&A Disobedient Objects

We're headed to the Disobedient Objects exhibition over the road at V&A and then to Eastside for a couple drinks. Admission is free. Meet at 6pm sharp in front of the Business School entrance.
